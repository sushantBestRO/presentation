Our Strategic Path to 25%+ Growth

Deepen Key Accounts

Action: Conduct quarterly business reviews and pitch at least one Value-Added Service (VAS) to top 25 clients.

Metric: +10-15% revenue from top 25.

Vertical Diversification

Action: Target new, high-growth industries like Renewables, Electronics, and Pharma with specialized SLAs.

Metric: Secure 5+ new anchor clients.

Service Bundling

Action: Proactively offer integrated solutions combining transport, warehousing, and distribution.

Metric: 25%+ clients using >1 service.

Build a Digital Funnel

Action: Implement a single-window CRM for lead scoring, automated follow-ups, and sales tracking.

Metric: Increase conversion rate by 20%+.

Strengthen Vendor Power

Action: Build a loyal vendor base through fair practices, timely payments, and bulk rate deals.

Metric: Reduce costs by 5-10%.

Network Expansion

Action: Strategically establish PTL networks and partnerships in the South and East regions.

Metric: Achieve pan-India service capability.
